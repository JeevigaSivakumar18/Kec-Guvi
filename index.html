About Kongu Engineering College

Established in 1984, Kongu Engineering College (KEC) stands as a premier autonomous institution in Perundurai, Tamil Nadu, India. Managed by the Kongu Vellalar Institute of Technology Trust, the college has been at the forefront of technical education for nearly four decades, consistently delivering excellence in engineering and technology education.

Situated in the serene environment of Perundurai, Erode district, our sprawling campus provides an ideal learning atmosphere that nurtures innovation, creativity, and academic rigor. The college is affiliated with Anna University, Chennai, and has earned its autonomous status, allowing us to design curricula that are both industry-relevant and research-oriented.

With a strong commitment to quality education, KEC offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various engineering disciplines. Our state-of-the-art laboratories, modern infrastructure, and experienced faculty ensure that students receive world-class education that prepares them for the challenges of the global industry.

The college's vision is to emerge as a center of excellence in technical education, fostering holistic development of students through value-based education, cutting-edge research, and strong industry partnerships.

About GUVI

An IIT Madras & IIM Ahmedabad Incubated Company

GUVI (Grab Your Vernacular Imprint) is India's leading online learning and skill development platform, incubated by IIT Madras and IIM Ahmedabad. Founded in 2014 and acquired by HCL Group in 2022, GUVI has revolutionized technical education by making it accessible in native languages.

With over 3 million learners and partnerships with 2000+ corporate entities, GUVI bridges the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. The platform offers comprehensive courses in software development, data science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and emerging technologies.

GUVI's mission aligns perfectly with preparing students for the rapidly evolving tech industry by providing practical, hands-on learning experiences that complement traditional academic curricula.

Faculty Development Programs

GUVI conducts comprehensive Faculty Development Programs (FDP) at Kongu Engineering College, empowering educators with the latest industry trends, teaching methodologies, and technological advancements. These programs ensure that faculty members stay updated with cutting-edge technologies and pedagogical approaches.

  • Regular training sessions on emerging technologies
  • Hands-on workshops on modern teaching tools
  • Industry best practices integration

Board of Studies Representation

GUVI's industry experts serve as members on Kongu Engineering College's Board of Studies, ensuring that the curriculum remains aligned with current industry requirements and future technology trends. This collaboration brings valuable industry insights into academic planning.

  • Strategic academic planning guidance

How GUVI Empowers Kongu Students

Transforming academic knowledge into career-ready skills

1

Industry-Ready Skill Development

Students gain access to comprehensive courses in high-demand technologies, ensuring they acquire skills that are directly applicable in the industry. The platform offers hands-on projects and real-world scenarios that prepare students for actual workplace challenges.

2

Practical Learning Platforms

Through platforms like CodeKata, WebKata, and IDE tools, students practice coding and problem-solving in an interactive environment. These tools provide instant feedback and unlimited practice opportunities to master programming concepts.

3

Interview Preparation & Placement Support

GUVI's Nutcracker program specifically focuses on interview preparation through mock assessments, coding challenges, and personalized guidance. Students benefit from access to HyreNet, an AI-powered assessment platform that simulates real interview conditions.

4

Industry Certifications

Students earn NEP-aligned certifications upon course completion, which are recognized by industry and add significant value to their resumes. These certifications validate their skills and knowledge in specific technology domains.

5

Access to Corporate Network

Through GUVI's network of 2000+ corporate partners including PayPal, Freshworks, Flipkart, and Tata Communications, students gain exposure to potential employers and placement opportunities at leading companies.

6

Flexible Self-Paced Learning

Students can learn at their own pace with GUVI's flexible course structure, allowing them to balance their academic commitments while acquiring additional skills. The platform is accessible 24/7, enabling learning anytime, anywhere.
